Getting In

Andy or Michelle will send you the door code and alarm code by text before your stay.

On Arrival
  • 1
    Use the code Andy or Michelle texted you to unlock the door.
  • 2
    Move quickly to the alarm panel — you have about 30 seconds before it sounds. See the Alarm section below.
  • 3
    Make yourself at home! 🌊
🔒
Please keep door codes private. Always lock the deadbolt when you leave, even for a quick trip to the beach.

Alarm System

The alarm code will be texted to you by Andy or Michelle before your stay.

Disabling the Alarm (Arrival)
  • 1
    Walk directly to the alarm panel — it will be beeping.
  • 2
    Enter the code Andy or Michelle sent you.
  • 3
    Press [OFF] or [DISARM]. The beeping will stop and a green light will appear.
Setting the Alarm (Departure)
  • 1
    Close and lock all windows and doors.
  • 2
    Enter your code and press [AWAY] or [ARM].
  • 3
    Exit through the front door within 60 seconds.
⚠️
False alarm? Re-enter your code immediately. If the siren keeps going, text Andy or Michelle right away.

Local Cape Cod Guide

Our favorite spots near the house — enjoy Cape Cod like a local!

🏊 Middle Pond — Right Out Front

Middle Pond is one of the three interconnected Indian Ponds — over 100 acres of clear, clean freshwater perfect for swimming, kayaking, and fishing. You're right on it!

The pond's access point for canoes and kayaks is at the end of Flume Avenue off Mystic Drive (from Route 149). Note: there's a 10 HP motor limit on the water.

Fish species include largemouth bass, yellow perch, chain pickerel, and pumpkinseed. Bring a rod!

📍 Open in Maps: Middle Pond Access, Flume Ave

🌊 Craigville Beach — Our Favorite

🏖️
Craigville Beach
997 Craigville Beach Rd, Centerville, MA 02632
About 10 minutes from the house. A beautiful, wide sandy beach on Nantucket Sound — warm, calm water, great for kids and families. Lifeguards on duty in summer. Full facilities including restrooms, outdoor showers, and beach chair rentals. Restaurants right across the street.

Parking: ~$25/day. Arrive before 9am on weekends to get a spot — it fills up fast!
Tip: Walk east along the shore for views of Squaw Island where the Kennedy family once vacationed.
~10 min drive Family-friendly Lifeguards
